Floré de Café

Founded by a group of passionate individuals who share a love for both coffee and the gentle artistry of flowers, Floré de Café began with a simple vision: to create a space where people can find warmth, inspiration, and peace in every cup.

Floré de Café is a flower-inspired café that blends the comforting aroma of coffee with the timeless beauty of blooms. Every drink we craft reflects a season, while every flower we offer carries a story—from purity and hope to love and resilience.
